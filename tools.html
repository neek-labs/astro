My Processing Steps

Here's a rough outline of my typical deep-sky image processing pipeline using PixInsight:

  1. 🔍 Subframe Selection – Evaluate subs for star quality and signal.
  2. 🧹 Calibration & Cosmetic Correction – BatchPreprocessing with darks, flats, and bias frames.
  3. 🧬 Star Alignment & Integration – Register and stack the cleanest subs.
  4. 🌌 Dynamic Background Extraction – Clean up gradients and vignetting.
  5. 🌈 Photometric Color Calibration – Match star colors to real-world data.
  6. ⚡ Noise Reduction & Stretching – TGVDenoise and HistogramTransformation magic.
  7. 🌟 Star Masking & Enhancement – StarNet++, star reduction, local contrast.
  8. 🖌️ Final Touches – Color boost, sharpening, cropping, export to TIFF/JPG.
